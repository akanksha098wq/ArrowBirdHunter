ARROW BIRD HUNTER-Game Design Doc
CORE MECHANICS:
- Player swips/drages to aim arrow direction
- tap to shoot
- 6-7 arrows per level
- Hit birds to gain points
LEVELS:
Round 1: 3 slow birds,easy targets,wide lay area
Round 2: 4 birds,mediumspeed,smaller area
Round 3: 6 birds,fast,challenging
Round 4: 7 birds,very fast ,obstacles apppear
Round 5: 7 birds, expert diffulty,moving obstacles
Round 6: Endless mode or boss level
SCORING:
- small bird: 10 points
- Medium bird: 20 points
- Large bird: 30 points
- Bonus : Hit multiple with 1 arrow - 50 points
UI:
-Level number
-Arrows remaining counter
-Current score
-High score (saved locally)
-Level completed screen
